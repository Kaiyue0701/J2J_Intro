Introduction

The Job-to-Job Flow Data (J2J data) produced by the Longitudinal Employer-Household Dynamics (LEHD) Program of the US Census Bureau could be used to identify labor mobility across industries and geographies. The J2J data of the Houston Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) could provide new insights to changing demographic and socioeconomic characteristics of region's population.

By understanding the demographics of workers moving in and out of the Houston MSA, as well as the origin-destination information on location and industry sector changes, we could improve our regional growth forecast model to reflect more accurate migration scenarios and employment structures.

Job-to-job moves are the primary activities to improve job matches between firms and workers and are important to a thriving economy. This application presents some of the essential information on the characters of job-to-job flows in the Houston MSA. The application contains the six following pages:

Employment Profile: This section provides some fundamental information about the current employment market in the Houston MSA, including total jobs, workforce demographics and employment by industry sectors. Job dynamics are shown regarding job status, types of hires/separations, and geographic levels of job-to-job flows.
Temporal Trend: This section shows temporal trends of job inflows, outflows and net flows to the Houston MSA since 2001 and some key demographics among job-to-job movers. The temporal trends are filtered by industry sectors.
Demographics: This section contains detailed job-to-job flow volumes by each demographic group. The data are stratified by year, type of job flows and destination industry.
Industry: This section shows detailed job-to-job flow volumes by each industry sector. The data are stratified by year, type of flows and key demographic groups. Year-to-year comparison can be made on specific industries by matching all other characters.
Geography: This section maps the top origin and destination metropolitans that have job-to-job moves to/from the Houston MSA.
Earnings: This section lists the earnings changes from job-to-job moved among different industries, demographic groups and type of flows.
